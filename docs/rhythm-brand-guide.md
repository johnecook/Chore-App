# Rhythm Brand Guide

## Brand Overview

Rhythm is a family responsibility platform that helps families build routines, responsibility, independence, and financial awareness.

Rhythm is not a chore app.

Rhythm is a family operating system that helps parents gradually transfer responsibility to their children through routines, chores, goals, allowance, savings, and self-directed growth.

Core Values:

- Responsibility
- Independence
- Consistency
- Trust
- Family Connection
- Simplicity

---

# Brand Personality

Rhythm should feel:

- Calm
- Trustworthy
- Modern
- Supportive
- Organized
- Family-oriented

Rhythm should NOT feel:

- Childish
- Gamified
- Corporate
- Overly playful
- Loud
- Competitive

---

# Positioning Statement

Helping families build responsibility, independence, and healthy routines.

---

# Tagline

Build responsibility together.

# Design Principles

1. Accessibility First
2. Minimal Visual Clutter
3. Large Touch Targets
4. Dark Mode First
5. Calm and Focused
6. Information Hierarchy Through Spacing
7. Family-Centered Experience

---

# Logo

Primary Logo:

- Navy circular badge
- White house outline
- Light aqua windows
- Two flowing wave lines beneath the house
- Rhythm wordmark beneath icon

Logo Meaning:

- House = Family
- Waves = Rhythm, routines, consistency
- Circle = Trust and unity

# Voice

Use language that is:

- Clear
- Positive
- Encouraging
- Family-oriented

Examples:

Good:
"3 tasks completed today"

Bad:
"You earned 3 achievement points!"

Good:
"Goal progress"

Bad:
"Level Up!"

Good:
"Waiting for approval"

Bad:
"Pending Reward Unlock"

# Product Mission
